Creating Your Own Web Page in GitHub

Follow the below steps to create your GitHub account

Step 1 - Get a username
Go to www.github.com and select sign up
Enter a username, email, and password
Step 2 - Create your repositories
Once logged in go look at your repositories, click new, and provide a name
Step 3 - Upload your files
Select "upload existing file"
Step 4 - Commit your changes
Once your files are uploaded, select "commit change"
Type your repository site in the browser to double check that it works
